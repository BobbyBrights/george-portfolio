Splotify

Latest music anywhere

Splotify gives you acces to the latest songs, albums, playlists and charts from leading sources and artists like Drake, John Mayer and many more. Exploring and discovering new music is mostly driven by your friends and taste. Splotify looks at your taste and friends and gives you the latest music for an unique listening experience.

Branding Splotify

Sometimes you can’t see what’s infront of you, sound is like that, music is like that and Splotify was exactly like that. Days passed by without knowing what to do, just untill the last moment. Sound has has shapes, music changes those shapes. Within a few hours I got the logo up.
